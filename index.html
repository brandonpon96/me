EcoWatch

Environmental issues are always a concern.

With our application, you can contribute to raising awareness of any environment issue by just taking a picture of what you see, be it a major litter area, oil spillage, heavy smog, or anything else. Along with the picture, your location will also be added to compiled data eco-map, where you can view where people are seeing environmental concerns around the world.

Kinematix

Ever wondered how you could make your physics class fun and interesting? Well look no further!

Kinematix is an android physics simulator app designed with students in mind. It is a sandbox type application that allows the users to create all kinds of scenarios, and have it be as simple or complicated as neccessary. After creating the scenario, just hit the play button and watch the action begin!
